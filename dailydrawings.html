My Daily Drawing Challenge!

288 Days. 288 Drawings.

Why 288? That's how many notes were in the stack I bought.

My Daily Drawing Challenge!

For the longest time I kept saying to myself, "I should draw more" but never actually did. So I finally decided to set myself a Daily Drawing Challenge. One complete drawing, finished before midnight, and no zero-days.

This turned out to be far more difficult than I had initially thought. The discipline needed to build up the routine and fight off the procrastination and perfectionism demons was a daily struggle.

I started by copying artwork from artists that I liked, trying to get it as close as possible. From Day 80 onwards, I got one word drawing prompts from a little app, and instead would reference photos after a quick Google search.

I'm glad I attempted it and thrilled that I accomplished it!
