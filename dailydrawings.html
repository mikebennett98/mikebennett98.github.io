My Daily Drawing Challenge

12 May 22 to 23 February 23

For the longest time I kept saying to myself, "I should draw more" but never actually did. So I set myself a Daily Drawing Challenge. One complete drawing finished before midnight and no zero days.
This turned out to be far more difficult than I had initially thought. The discipline needed to build up the routine and fight off the procrastination and perfectionism demons was a daily struggle.
I'm glad I attempted it and thrilled that I accomplished it.

288 Days. 288 Drawings.

Why 288? That's how many notes were in the stack I bought.
