Hey I'm Michael!
An aspiring creative professional currently working as a junior graphic designer in Durban for the past 3 years.

After graduating as 'student of the year' from Keyline Digital Design College, I set to work at HUL International, a home furnishings wholesaler, designing everything from rugs and cushions to home fragrances packaging and gifting for some of South Africas biggest home retailers.

I pride myself on a high quality of work, if you're going to do something, you do it right. Simpler said than done, during the creation phase, I like to try out different variations and iterations of design elements to find the solution that works best.

It's still early in my career so I haven't found my niche or built up an expertise in a particular field of design yet but I'm enjoying the process in getting there!
